## Methodology

**Cost score** = (total loadable lines / 50) + (chains × 2) + (verify loops × 1) + (sub-agents × 3)

- **Size**: Lines in `skill.md`
- **Aux**: Lines in `examples.md` + `reference.md` (loaded on demand)
- **References**: Files in `references/` subdirectory (count/total lines)
- **Chains To**: Number of other skills referenced (each loads additional context)
- **Verify Loops**: Patterns like re-read, re-fetch, post-update checks
- **Sub-Agents**: Patterns dispatching fresh agents (think-twice consultant, etc.)

**Tiers**: low (<8), medium (8-14.9), high (≥15)

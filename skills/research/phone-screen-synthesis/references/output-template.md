## << Observation >> Convention

Use `<< >>` for inline observations:
- Real-time gut reactions
- Behavioral notes (energy, hesitation)
- Positive signals or concerns

Preserve raw interviewer voice. Do NOT sanitize.

**Do NOT include:** Meta-commentary about the interviewer, analysis of interviewer behavior. Reader evaluates the CANDIDATE.

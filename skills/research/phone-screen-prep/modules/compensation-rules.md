# Compensation Discussion Rules

## 🚨 COMPENSATION DISCUSSION RULES

<EXTREMELY_IMPORTANT>

**Agency candidates and direct-apply candidates require DIFFERENT handling:**

| Candidate Source | Comp Discussion | Why |
|------------------|-----------------|-----|
| **Agency-sourced** | ❌ **SKIP ENTIRELY** | Agency has already briefed candidate on our base-only structure, salary range, and negotiated on their behalf |
| **Direct-apply** | ✅ **MANDATORY** | Candidate has not been briefed — must confirm base-only, range alignment, expectations |

### Agency-Sourced Candidates

**DO NOT:**
- Ask salary expectations
- Explain base-only structure
- Discuss comp range
- Include ANY comp confirmation section (not even "N/A")

**DO:**
- Add agency callout box at top of file
- Set Source field to `🏢 Agency Name (Agency)`
- Focus call time on technical depth and culture fit

### Direct-Apply Candidates

**MUST:**
- Confirm salary expectation vs. our $140-180k range
- Explain base-only structure (no bonus, no equity)
- Include comp confirmation section with script
- Gate early if major misalignment

**Rationale:** Agency has already vetted comp fit — re-discussing wastes time and can create confusion. Direct applicants have no prior briefing and need explicit alignment.

</EXTREMELY_IMPORTANT>

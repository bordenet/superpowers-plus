# Interview Integrity Policy Scripts

> **Reference:** [AI & Interview Integrity Policy](https://wiki.int.[company].net/doc/ai-interview-integrity-policy-U0AU2Qv67Q)

## 🚨 MANDATORY — Two Scripts Required

**Two scripts are REQUIRED in every phone screen:**

---

## 1. Start of Call (after intro, before questions)

> *"Before we dive in, I need to cover our interview integrity policy. We don't allow AI assistance during any part of the interview process — phone screen, technical interviews, behavioral rounds, any of it. If you have a disability that requires assistive technology, now is the time to tell me and we'll work something out. After this call, no new disclosures. Does that make sense? Any questions or anything you need to disclose?"*

**Checkboxes in template:**
- [ ] Policy read
- [ ] Accommodations disclosed? _______________

---

## 2. End of Call (if advancing to loop)

> *"Before we wrap up — a few things about our loop process. First, the interview integrity policy I mentioned at the start applies to every session. No AI assistance, join on time. If you no-show without notice, we close the application. Second, if you have any assistive technology needs you didn't mention earlier, now is the last chance to disclose. And third — if you make it through the loop, the final interview is in-person at our Dallas office. We cover travel for candidates outside DFW. Any questions about any of that?"*

**Checkboxes in template:**
- [ ] Loop expectations communicated
- [ ] Final accommodations check: _______________
- [ ] Dallas in-person acknowledged

---

## When to Use Each Script

| Script | When | Skip If |
|--------|------|---------|
| Start of Call | ALWAYS — no exceptions | Never skip |
| End of Call | Only if advancing to loop | NO-HIRE decision |

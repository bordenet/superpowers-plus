# Evidence Tracking During Call

**While taking Running Notes, mark sources for easier synthesis:**

```markdown
**Raw Observations:**
- [transcript] "I do really well figuring things out on my own" — her exact words
- [observation] Energy increased when discussing mentorship
- [resume-verify] Confirmed VideoAmp was ad-tech (Nielsen competitor) — discussed in call
- [resume-unverified] 13+ years — not discussed, from resume only
- [question] Asked about team size — reveals interest in small team
```

**Why this matters:**
- Synthesis skill requires source attribution for every claim
- Mixing resume + transcript evidence without tags = audit failure
- Post-call you'll forget what was discussed vs. assumed

**Tags to use:**

| Tag | Meaning |
|-----|---------|
| `[transcript]` | Direct quote from candidate |
| `[observation]` | Your real-time assessment |
| `[resume-verify]` | Resume claim confirmed in call |
| `[resume-unverified]` | Resume claim NOT discussed |
| `[question]` | Their question to you |
